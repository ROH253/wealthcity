What is Wealth City?

Wealth City is a financial literacy app designed for teens to learn about managing money in a fun and engaging way. With gamified lessons and rewards, you'll master personal finance skills and build healthy financial habits.

Our Core Features

Gamification

Complete challenges and quizzes to earn points and rewards.

Interactive Dashboard

Track your savings, expenses, and investments in real-time.

Learning Modules

Engaging lessons on finance basics to boost your knowledge.

Rewards System

Convert rewards into real investments when you turn 18.
